Our Story

Our story unfolds with a shared passion for customising mechanical keyboards, uniting a group of like-minded hobbyists. The inception of our shop was sparked when our collective experiences, discomfort due to prolonged typing with unsuitable keyboards, propelled us into the realm of seeking more ergonomic solutions. Identifying a void in the local market for mechanical keyboard customisation, we saw an opportunity to pool our expertise and establish a hub for enthusiasts.

Fueled by a desire to make quality keyboards more accessible and to streamline the cumbersome process of sourcing components from overseas, our collective vision materialised into the founding of our mechanical keyboard shop.

Join us in celebrating the art of customisation and the pursuit of a more comfortable and enjoyable typing experience.
